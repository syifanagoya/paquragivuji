Paul Hollywood | Career

Paul attended The Mosslands School in his hometown of Wallasey.

He then enrolled at the Wallasey School of Art to study sculpting but dropped out midway to pursue a career as a baker.

Before working in other bakeries, he worked at his father’s England bakery. Eventually, he would work his way up to become the chief baker at a few hotels.

In 2015, Hollywood made his racing debut with Andrew Howard, the founder of Beechdean Dairies, in the Beechdean AMR Aston Martin Vantage GT4.

FAQs

What did Paul’s father and grandfather do?

Paul is the third generation of bakers in his family. Paul’s father established the Bread Winner bakery business, eventually extending from Aberdeen to Lincolnshire. Paul’s grandpa worked as the head baker at the Adelphi Hotel in Liverpool.

Is Paul Hollywood colorblind?

Yes, Paul confessed during a segment of the show that he is colorblind.

Is Paul Hollywood’s eye real?

His eyes aren’t lenses; they’re completely Paul’s. He got them from his grandfather. Norman Haman, one of Hollywood’s grandfathers, served in the army during WWII.
